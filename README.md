# Full & Final(F&F) Settlement ⚙️ Automation with 🤝🏻 Workflow Orchestration & 📜 Compliance Framework 💼

Designed and implemented a comprehensive Full & Final (F&F) Settlement solution in SAP HCM to streamline employee offboarding processes. The solution automates settlement calculations, integrates workflow approvals, and ensures accurate processing of payouts and recoveries while enhancing transparency, compliance, and employee experience.

![ABAP](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nimi-soni/FAF/refs/heads/main/FAF/FAF%20(1).png)

**Key Functionalities**:
* Developed a custom F&F settlement program to automate end-to-end offboarding financial processing.
* Designed and configured a multi-level approval workflow involving:
    * 🧑🏻‍💻 Initiator (HR/Admin)
    * 👨🏻‍💼 Verifier
    * ✍🏻 Approver (final authorization) 
* Created a custom Infotype to capture and track complete F&F settlement data, including:
    * 🧑🏻‍💼 Employee details (designation, level, DOJ, LWD, cost center)
    * 💰 Payroll-related data (last payroll run, basic pay, leave encashment days)
    * ⚖️ Compliance details (PAN, UAN, location)
* Automated complex settlement calculations, covering:
    * 💸 Leave encashment
    * ⏳ Notice period recovery
    * 💵 LWOP (Loss of Pay)
    * 🏆 Gratuity / final payouts
    * ⚒️ Multiple recoveries (canteen, handset, SIM, laptop damage, medical, etc.) 
* Enabled dynamic data retrieval from SAP HR master and payroll data sources. 
* Developed multiple Smart Forms (based on employee categories) for structured F&F statement generation. 
* Built driver program to process data, execute calculations, and trigger form generation. 
* Integrated SAP Workflow and Email Notifications for:
    * ⭐ Submission, approval, and rejection steps
    * 🕒 Real-time status updates to stakeholders
* Ensured validation and verification layer before final approval to maintain data integrity. 

![ABAP](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nimi-soni/FAF/refs/heads/main/FAF/FAF%20(2).png)

**Business Impact**:
* Significantly reduced manual effort and human errors in complex F&F calculations.
* Accelerated employee offboarding process, improving overall employee experience.
* Delivered transparent and itemized settlement breakdowns for employees and auditors.
* Strengthened compliance and audit readiness with system-driven tracking and approvals.
* Eliminated data duplication and inconsistencies through real-time synchronization.
* Reduced administrative overhead and processing costs associated with manual workflows.
* Enabled structured, scalable offboarding framework adaptable across employee categories.
